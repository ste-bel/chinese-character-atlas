W0017 — 说

1 🔊

Pronunciation

说 Pinyin: shuō Tone: First tone (high and level) Meaning: to say; to speak; to tell; to explain; speech

Traditional form: 說

The initial consonant 'sh' is retroflex — the tongue curls back. This is different from the English 'sh' in "she."

2 📖

Core Usage

说 is the primary verb for speaking and saying in Mandarin. It is used when quoting speech, reporting what someone said, and discussing language ability.

说 + Language → to speak [language] 说 + Quoted content → to say [something] 说明 → to explain; explanation

3 ✎

Examples

她说中文说得很好。 Tā shuō Zhōngwén shuō de hěn hǎo. She speaks Chinese very well. Literal: She / speak / Chinese / speak / [manner complement] / very / well.

他说他不来了。 Tā shuō tā bù lái le. He said he's not coming. Literal: He / said / he / not / come / [change of state].

你在说什么？ Nǐ zài shuō shénme? What are you saying? Literal: You / in the process of / say / what?

4 ？

Question

你会说中文吗？ Nǐ huì shuō Zhōngwén ma? Can you speak Chinese?

✅ 会说一点儿。 Huì shuō yīdiǎnr. I can speak a little.

❌ 不会说。 Bù huì shuō. I can't speak it.

5 ❖

Common Compounds

说话 — to speak; to talk (general) 说明 — to explain; explanation; instructions 小说 — novel (fiction) 传说 — legend; folklore 听说 — to hear (that); it is said that

◆ Atlas Note

说 contains 言 (speech/words) as its semantic component — the part that carries meaning — and 兑 as its phonetic component, which historically provided sound guidance. This is the logic of the vast majority of Chinese characters: one part suggests meaning, one part suggests sound. 说 is a perfect example of this phono-semantic structure.
