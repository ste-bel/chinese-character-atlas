W0006 — 人

1 🔊

Pronunciation

人 Pinyin: rén Tone: Second tone (rising) Meaning: person; people; human being

2 📖

Core Usage

人 is both singular and plural in Chinese. Context determines number.

一个人 — one person 很多人 — many people / a lot of people 中国人 — Chinese person/people

人 also combines with other words to form words about types of people:

商人 — merchant 工人 — worker 外国人 — foreigner

3 ✎

Examples

她是一个好人。 Tā shì yīgè hǎo rén. She is a good person. Literal: She / is / one / [measure word] / good / person.

这里有很多人。 Zhèlǐ yǒu hěnduō rén. There are many people here. Literal: Here / have / very many / people.

你是哪国人？ Nǐ shì nǎ guó rén? What country are you from? Literal: You / are / which / country / person?

4 ？

Question

你是中国人吗？ Nǐ shì Zhōngguó rén ma? Are you Chinese?

✅ 是的，我是中国人。 Shì de, wǒ shì Zhōngguó rén. Yes, I am Chinese.

❌ 不，我不是中国人。 Bù, wǒ bú shì Zhōngguó rén. No, I'm not Chinese.

5 ⚠

Common Mistake

人 does not require a measure word when used generically. Use 个 when counting specific people:

✅ 人很多 (People are many / There are many people) ✅ 三个人 (three people) ❌ 三人 (archaic or formal register only)

◆ Atlas Note

人 is one of the oldest and most pictographic characters in Chinese. The original oracle bone form shows a person bending forward — a simple side view of a human figure. Nearly every Chinese concept about humanity, society, and relationships includes 人.
