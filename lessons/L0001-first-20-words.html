Lesson 1 — The First 20 Words

These are among the most frequently used words in modern Mandarin, ordered by descending corpus frequency. The rank is each character's position in Jun Da's Modern Chinese Character Frequency List — see data/word-frequency-reference.md. Mastering these words gives you a foundation present in the majority of everyday Chinese sentences.

Patterns These 20 Words Unlock

With just these 20 words you can form:

Statements: 我是学生。I am a student. 他不来了。He is not coming. 这是我的。This is mine.

Questions: 你是哪国人？What country are you from? 你有时间吗？Do you have time? 为什么？Why?

Key grammar:

  • 是 — identification (A is B)
  • 不 — negation (not; no)
  • 的 — possession and description
  • 了 — completion and change of state
  • 有 — possession and existence
  • 在 — location
