# Fire Horse 3D Logo — Production Brief

## Identity

The animated 3D logo must preserve two separate assets:

1. **Atlas Logo**
   - The round Fire Horse emblem.
   - Used for site branding, loading screen, video intro, app icon, favicon.

2. **Author Signature**
   - The personal name seal for Stéphane Bélanger:
   - **白朗志远**
   - Pinyin: **Bái Lǎng Zhìyuǎn**
   - Meaning: bright, clear aspiration reaching far.

These are not interchangeable.

## Critical rule

Do not let any image model invent Chinese characters.

All Chinese text must be supplied as verified vector/text assets.

The final personal seal must read exactly:

白朗志远

## Preferred visual direction

Use the first 3D Fire Horse image as the emotional direction:

- carved bronze / stone medallion
- dark cinematic background
- living flame
- glowing carved character
- realistic Fire Horse with flowing mane
- embers and volumetric light
- premium museum feel

## Animation concept

Loop length: 6–8 seconds.

1. Darkness and smoke.
2. Embers appear.
3. Fire circle ignites.
4. Horse emerges from flame.
5. Seal character glows.
6. Personal name seal briefly stamps in red.
7. Fire calms to a steady living burn.

## Technical deliverables

- 4K hero animation
- Transparent logo loop
- Square app icon
- Static PNG fallback
- SVG/vector name seal
- Dark and light versions
- Web-optimized MP4/WebM
- GIF preview
- Poster frame

## Production pipeline

Recommended:

1. Create verified vector seal for 白朗志远.
2. Build 3D medallion from that vector.
3. Animate fire and horse.
4. Composite lighting and embers.
5. Export web and video variants.

## Do not

- Do not use AI-generated Chinese text in final assets.
- Do not distort the seal.
- Do not flatten the horse into a cartoon.
- Do not make it look like a game logo.
- Do not remove the ancient/premium feeling.
