2 ❖

The Name 白朗志远

◆ Atlas Note

A Chinese name is not a translation. It is a new identity constructed from meaningful characters chosen to reflect character, aspiration, and origin.

白 (bái) — white; pure; bright; clear Surname Bai. Phonetic echo of Bé- in Bélanger.

朗 (lǎng) — bright; clear; open; cheerful Inner character. From Zhuge Liang's Admonitions to My Son (诫子书): 光明磊落 — bright and open.

志 (zhì) — aspiration; will; ambition; purpose Inner strength. Goal: a far-reaching future.

远 (yuǎn) — far; distant; long-term The horizon. 目标：遠大前程.

Together: Bright and upright, with lofty aspirations, moving steadily toward great distances.

3 ❖

Fire Horse · 火马之人

Born in 丙午年 — the Year of the Fire Horse.

In Chinese astrology, the Fire Horse is rare, appearing only once every sixty years. Those born under this sign are said to be bold, independent, and relentlessly forward-moving.

志存高遠 — Hold lofty aspirations. Aspire high.

4 ❖

Motto

From B0001 诫子书 — Admonitions to My Son by Zhuge Liang, Chancellor of Shu Han:

非淡泊无以明志，非宁静无以致远。

Fēi dànbó wúyǐ míng zhì, fēi níngjìng wúyǐ zhì yuǎn.

Without simplicity and detachment, one cannot make one's aspirations clear; without tranquility, one cannot reach far.
