# Atlas Bible 2.0

## Mission
Build the world's finest Chinese language encyclopedia.

## Principles
- Every character tells a story.
- Every word opens a door.
- Beauty improves learning.
- Curiosity is the primary navigation.
- Markdown is the source of truth.
- Knowledge is interconnected.
- Historical accuracy is mandatory.
- Engineering serves education.
- Timelessness is preferred over fashion.

## Audience
Beginners, advanced learners, teachers, linguists, translators, historians and native speakers.

## Success
A visitor arrives to learn one word and leaves having explored Chinese language, history, literature and culture.
