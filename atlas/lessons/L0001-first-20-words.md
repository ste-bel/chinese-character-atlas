# Lesson 1 — The First 20 Words

These are the 20 most frequently used words in modern Mandarin, ordered by corpus frequency. Mastering these 20 words gives you a foundation present in the majority of everyday Chinese sentences.

## Patterns These 20 Words Unlock

With just these 20 words you can form:

**Statements:**
我是学生。I am a student.
他不来了。He is not coming.
这是我的。This is mine.

**Questions:**
你是哪国人？What country are you from?
你有时间吗？Do you have time?
为什么？Why?

**Key grammar:**
- [[W0001|是]] — identification (A is B)
- [[W0004|不]] — negation (not; no)
- [[W0002|的]] — possession and description
- [[W0005|了]] — completion and change of state
- [[W0008|有]] — possession and existence
- [[W0007|在]] — location
