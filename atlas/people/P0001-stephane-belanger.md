# 白朗志远

## Author

Stéphane Bélanger is the creator of the Chinese Character Atlas.

He studies Mandarin as an adult learner, approaching the language not only as a
system of communication but as an archive of civilization — a living record of
how millions of people across thousands of years understood the world.

His Chinese name is **白朗志远** — given, not chosen at random.

---

## The Name 白朗志远

{{atlas-note}}
A Chinese name is not a translation. It is a new identity constructed from
meaningful characters chosen to reflect character, aspiration, and origin.
{{/atlas-note}}

**白** (bái) — white; pure; bright; clear
Surname Bai. Phonetic echo of *Bé-* in Bélanger.

**朗** (lǎng) — bright; clear; open; cheerful
Inner character. From Zhuge Liang's *Admonitions to My Son* (诫子书): 光明磊落 — bright and open.

**志** (zhì) — aspiration; will; ambition; purpose
Inner strength. Goal: a far-reaching future.

**远** (yuǎn) — far; distant; long-term
The horizon. 目标：遠大前程.

Together: **Bright and upright, with lofty aspirations, moving steadily toward great distances.**

## Fire Horse · 火马之人

Born in 丙午年 — the Year of the Fire Horse.

In Chinese astrology, the Fire Horse is rare, appearing only once every sixty
years. Those born under this sign are said to be bold, independent, and
relentlessly forward-moving.

志存高遠 — *Hold lofty aspirations. Aspire high.*

---

## Motto

From [[B0001]] — *Admonitions to My Son* by Zhuge Liang, Chancellor of Shu Han:

> 非淡泊无以明志，非宁静无以致远。

*Fēi dànbó wúyǐ míng zhì, fēi níngjìng wúyǐ zhì yuǎn.*

Without simplicity and detachment, one cannot make one's aspirations clear;
without tranquility, one cannot reach far.
