# W0014 — 国

## Pronunciation

**国**
**Pinyin:** guó
**Tone:** Second tone (rising)
**Meaning:** country; nation; national; of or pertaining to a country

**Traditional form:** 國

## Core Usage

国 is primarily a component in compound words. It rarely stands alone. It combines with other characters to name countries, national concepts, and national identities.

中国 — China
美国 — USA (Beautiful Country)
英国 — UK (Heroic Country)
法国 — France (Law Country)
德国 — Germany (Virtue Country)

## Country Names

The Chinese names for countries are often poetic or phonetic transliterations with meaningful characters:

| Country | Chinese | Pinyin | Literal meaning |
|---------|---------|--------|-----------------|
| China | 中国 | Zhōngguó | Middle Kingdom |
| USA | 美国 | Měiguó | Beautiful Country |
| UK | 英国 | Yīngguó | Heroic Country |
| France | 法国 | Fǎguó | Law Country |
| Germany | 德国 | Déguó | Virtue Country |
| Japan | 日本 | Rìběn | Sun's Origin (not 国) |

## Examples

{{audio:你是哪国人？}}
你是哪国人？
*Nǐ shì nǎ guó rén?*
What country are you from?
Literal: You / are / which / country / person?

{{audio:我是加拿大人，不是美国人。}}
我是加拿大人，不是美国人。
*Wǒ shì Jiānádà rén, bú shì Měiguó rén.*
I am Canadian, not American.

{{audio:这是哪国的？}}
这是哪国的？
*Zhè shì nǎ guó de?*
Which country is this from?

## Common Compounds

国家 — country; nation; state
国际 — international
出国 — to go abroad
爱国 — to love one's country; patriotic
全国 — the whole country; nationwide
国王 — king (king of a country)

## Atlas Note

{{atlas-note}}
The traditional character 國 shows a jade (or person) enclosed within a boundary — a territory with something precious inside. The simplified 国 reduces the interior to 玉 (jade) — still preserving the sense of something of value within borders. The character captures an ancient idea: a country is a bounded space containing something worth protecting.
{{/atlas-note}}
