# W0011 — 中

## Pronunciation

**中**
**Pinyin:** zhōng / zhòng
**Tone:** First tone (zhōng) or Fourth tone (zhòng)
**Meaning:**
- zhōng — middle; center; China; in the process of
- zhòng — to hit (a target); to be hit by; to win (a prize)

This entry covers **zhōng** — the far more common pronunciation.

## Core Usage as 中 (zhōng)

中 carries the core meaning of *middle*, *center*, and *within*. From this come several important uses:

**中国** — China (Middle Kingdom)
**中文** — Chinese language (written)
**中间** — between; in the middle
**中午** — noon (middle of the day)
**正在中** — in the middle of [doing something]

## 中 in 中国

中国 (Zhōngguó) — China — literally means "Middle Kingdom" or "Central Country." This name reflects the historical Chinese worldview in which China occupied the center of the known world. The name dates back thousands of years and originally referred to the central states of the Zhou dynasty.

## Atlas Note

{{atlas-note}}
中 is not just a word — it is a concept deeply embedded in Chinese philosophy and aesthetics. The idea of the middle path, balance, and centrality appears in Confucian thought (中庸, the Doctrine of the Mean), in the name of China itself, and in countless everyday expressions. To learn 中 is to touch something fundamental about how Chinese civilization has understood the world.
{{/atlas-note}}
