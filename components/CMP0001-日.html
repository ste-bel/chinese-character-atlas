CMP0001 — 日

1 ❖

Overview

Character: 日

Pinyin: rì

Meaning: sun; day; time; brightness

Origin

日 is one of the oldest Chinese characters.

It began as a pictograph of the sun, originally drawn as a circle with a dot in the middle. Over thousands of years it gradually became the square form used today.

Early forms were more rounded and often had a mark inside representing the sun's centre.

Cultural Significance

Because the sun marks daytime and the cycle of time, 日 became associated with day, date, light, and time-related meanings.

The sun governs:

  • daylight
  • time
  • seasons
  • agriculture
  • life

Because of this, 日 appears in many characters related to light, time, and celestial phenomena.

Common Confusions

日 — sun

曰 — to say

目 — eye

Although similar in appearance, they have different origins and meanings.
