# Component — 日

**Pinyin:** rì  
**Meaning:** sun; day; time; brightness

## Origin

日 began as a pictograph of the sun. Early forms were more rounded and often had a mark inside representing the sun’s center.

## Significance

Because the sun marks daytime and the cycle of time, 日 became associated with day, date, light, and time-related meanings.

## Characters using 日

- 是
- 明
- 时
- 晚
- 昨
- 星
