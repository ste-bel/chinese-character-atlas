# W0001 — 是

**Pinyin:** shì  
**Meaning:** to be; is; are; am; yes; correct

## Usage

是 is the basic linking verb in Modern Mandarin: A is B.

## Important learner note

Chinese does not normally use 是 before simple adjectives.

✅ 我很好。  
*Wǒ hěn hǎo.*  
I am good.

❌ 我是很好。  
Unnatural in normal beginner usage.

## Example

我是加拿大人。  
*Wǒ shì Jiānádà rén.*  
I am Canadian.  
Literal: I / am / Canada / person.

## Question

你是加拿大人吗？  
*Nǐ shì Jiānádà rén ma?*  
Are you Canadian?

✅ 是，我是。  
*Shì, wǒ shì.*  
Yes, I am.

❌ 不是，我不是。  
*Bú shì, wǒ bú shì.*  
No, I am not.

## New words used

- 我 — I, me
- 你 — you
- 加拿大 — Canada
- 人 — person
- 吗 — question particle

## Components

- [[日]] — sun; day

## Notes

Classical Chinese used 是 differently from Modern Mandarin. Later entries should distinguish historical usage from modern grammar.
