CMP0001 — 日

Overview

Character: 日

Pinyin: rì

Meaning: sun; day

Origin

日 is one of the oldest Chinese characters.

It began as a pictograph of the sun, originally drawn as a circle with a dot in the middle. Over thousands of years it gradually became the square form used today.

Cultural Significance

The sun governs:

  • daylight
  • time
  • seasons
  • agriculture
  • life

Because of this, 日 appears in many characters related to light, time, and celestial phenomena.

Common Confusions

日 — sun

曰 — to say

目 — eye

Although similar in appearance, they have different origins and meanings.
